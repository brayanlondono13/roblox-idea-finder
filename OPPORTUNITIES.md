# Roblox Build-Next Opportunity Report
### Slime-RNG x Cooking/Craft-Food — where the open 1k+ lanes are

**Audience:** a builder who loves *Slime RNG* (roll → collect → upgrade → unlock-worlds) and cooking/craft-food games, hunting an **open** niche that can sustain **1,000+ CCU**.

**Bottom line:** The cleanest white space sits where a **proven RNG/incremental engine** is reskinned into **food/cooking** — because the *cooking and restaurant niches are genuinely fragmented* (no monolith), while the *RNG/incremental engine* is what actually reaches 20k–140k CCU. The winning move is to take the loop that scales (gacha-roll incremental or restaurant tycoon) and skin it in food, **not** to bet on the cooking-craft step as the demand driver.

> **Read this first (honesty note):** every CCU figure below is a **single live snapshot** (no peak/retention history), and the headline niche HHI/leader-share numbers are computed over keyword-search clusters that include bleed-through mega-titles. I have de-contaminated the analysis by anchoring to per-`genre_l2` breakdowns and individual game rows, exactly where the methodology review says to trust the data. See the caveats box at the end.

## 3) Top picks — loop, 1k proof, differentiation, biggest risk

### Pick 1 — Sell Lemonade Tycoon: Roll the Recipe  *(adjusted confidence: HIGH)*
- **Core loop:** Run a food/drink stand → **roll** random recipes + ingredient mutations (Golden Lemon, Rainbow Boba) that multiply sale price → place auto-serving stations (offline cash) → reinvest in stations/luck/new themes (Lemonade → Boba → Ramen) → rebirth to scale.
- **Why 1k+ is feasible (fresh proof):** *Sell Lemons* proves the food-stand fantasy at **~99,015 CCU**; **Build A Ring Farm** (grow-sell incremental with a mutation layer) hit **39,724 CCU at just 61 days**. The mutation-multiplier hook is the exact pattern that game uses.
- **Differentiation:** Injects rarity-chasing into a flat cash-tycoon — the dopamine spike *Sell Lemons* lacks, while keeping the chill stand fantasy. No food-tycoon-with-RNG-roller exists in the data.
- **Biggest risk (from verification):** The **exact fusion has zero proven incumbent** — both parents are independently proven but the combo is untested execution risk; the offline-tycoon and rarity-chase loops must be balanced so neither cannibalizes the other.

### Pick 2 — Roll-a-Dish: Recipe Gacha Kitchen  *(adjusted confidence: MEDIUM)*
- **Core loop:** Tap a **Mystery Burner** to roll a random dish (common→mythic, visible luck stat) → placed dishes auto-cook and earn offline → spend on better burners (rarity odds), luck potions, new kitchen-worlds (Diner → Sushi Bar → Space Cafe) → rebirth for permanent luck/multipliers → recipe-dex completion grants income buffs. **It is Slime RNG with dishes instead of slimes.**
- **Why 1k+ is feasible (fresh proof):** *Catch and Cook!* hit **~1,337 CCU at 102 days** using exactly this skeleton (RNG-catch + cook + offline + luck + 1.8M combos). *Dumpling Squishy Race* (Incremental Sim) hit **5,440 CCU in 32 days**, proving fresh entrants break 1k fast in this genre.
- **Differentiation:** *Catch and Cook* gates the RNG behind a fishing minigame and is fish-only; this leads with the **pure slot-machine roll on all food types**, a collectible recipe-dex with set bonuses, and a more generous gacha than any current cooking incremental.
- **Biggest risk (from verification):** **Soft ceiling.** The only direct roll-gacha-on-food comp, *Catch and Cook!*, tops out at ~1,337 CCU with a mediocre 86.9% rating — the pure-RNG cooking loop clusters at ~1.3k, not 5k+. 1k is achievable but not comfortably exceeded; success leans on a more dopamine-rich gacha and execution.

### Pick 3 — Baddie Bistro: Collect Chefs, Cook & Rebirth  *(adjusted confidence: MEDIUM)*
- **Core loop:** Roll randomized **chef** units (escalating rarity) → assign to cooking stations (higher rarity = faster/rarer dishes) → customers auto-pay, income accrues offline → spend on roll tickets, luck/pets, station upgrades, expansions → rebirth for permanent luck/cash multipliers → chef-dex completion for buffs.
- **Why 1k+ is feasible (fresh proof):** *Run a Restaurant!* (**19,363 CCU @75d**) and *My Gaming Cafe!* (**4,011 @32d**) prove fresh offline-serve tycoons clear 1k fast. The exact hybrid already prototypes as *My Baddie Restaurant! 🎲* at **852 CCU @59d** with a standout **0.275 ccu-per-1k-visits** (high stickiness) — on the cusp of 1k with a thin build.
- **Differentiation:** Incumbents (*Run a Restaurant*, *RT3*) are pure build/serve tycoons with **no gacha**. This injects the Slime-RNG collection spine (roll rare chefs, luck, pets, dex). Out-polish and out-content the rough prototype.
- **Biggest risk (from verification):** The **RNG-chef + tycoon fusion is unproven at 1k+** — the single direct precedent sits at **852 CCU (below the bar)**, and every game *above* 1k here is a pure tycoon with no gacha. The bet hinges on gacha *lifting* the game past entrenched pure-tycoon incumbents rather than just adding monetization friction.

## 4) Traps & cautions

**Niches that look open but are monoliths once the mega-title bleed is removed:**
- **Unboxing (HHI 7,648, leader 87.4%)** — that 87% is entirely *[UPD] +1 Speed Keyboard Escape | Candy & Chocolate* (248,197 CCU), a keyword-bleed title in the wrong genre. The *real* dumpling Tycoon sub-lane is fragmented, **but** see *Cook a Squishy Dumpling* below.
- **Merge (HHI 5,525)** — genuine concentration: *Merge a Nuke!* owns 73%, and the **Match & Merge sub-genre has only ONE 1k winner** (*Merge Shop*, 88.5% share — which is *already* a merge-food-and-serve game). Entering merge means fighting a single-game monolith that occupies your exact differentiation.
- **Garden (HHI 4,000, leader 58.4%)** — *Grow a Garden 2* owns 58% (481k CCU). The garden>Incremental sub-lane the "Roll a Recipe Garden" concept targeted is a **2,198-CCU desert** with one stale 329-day-old winner (*Bee Garden*, 1,158, 52.7% of the lane).

**Rejected concepts and why:**
- **Roll a Recipe Garden — REJECTED.** Chose the garden>Incremental lane whose *total* demand across all 16 games is **2,198 CCU**, with only one 1k winner (*Bee Garden*, 329d old, half the lane). The crossbreed/recipe-discovery hook is *already* shipping in sub-520-CCU garden games (*Grow Anything* 519, *Grow Brainrot Garden*) **without converting** — a demand desert, not a green field.
- **Capsule Chefs: Sort & Serve — REJECTED.** Targets **Match & Merge**, a true single-game monolith (*Merge Shop* 88.5%, which is already merge-food-and-serve). The "open" claim is borrowed entirely from a **19-day-old tidy/sort viral spike** (*Librarian* 17,887 @19d) that lives in a different genre, has no economy, and zero proven retention. Over-stacks RNG-unbox + sort + cook + serve + offline.

**Build-worthy but watch the trend clock:**
- **Cook a Squishy Dumpling** survived verification (the dumpling Tycoon sub-lane *is* fragmented — leader *Run for Squishy Dumpling!* only 29.6% / 4,778 CCU, with *Dumpling Squishy Race* 5,471 @32d). **But** the named incumbent *Squishy Dumpling Unboxing* (4,264 CCU) already runs the exact pitched loop, the cooking delta is untested on this meta, and reskin waves cycle fast — the trend may decay before launch.

**Over-scoping warning that recurs across picks:** the proven fresh 1k+ winners (*Kick a Lucky Block*, *Lucky Block Rush*, *Build A Ring Farm*) all win on a **single tight hook with instant dopamine**. Concepts that bolt on a brew/craft/combine step (*Lucky Pot RNG*, *Slime Kitchen RNG*) insert friction exactly where proven titles stay frictionless. Keep the roll-to-reward loop instant; make cooking a *flex/collection* layer, not a gate.

## 5) Tool & methodology caveats (how much to trust the numbers)

> - **Snapshot only.** Every CCU is a single live reading with no peak/retention (D1/D7) history. A game spiking during an event reads identically to a steadily-retained one. Treat all "winner" and "fresh winner" counts as **demand signals, not proof of durability.**
> - **Keyword contamination is real.** Headline niche HHI/leader-share is computed over a keyword-search cluster that mixes genres and includes bleed-through mega-titles (e.g. *+1 Speed Keyboard Escape* shows up as the "leader" of incremental/unboxing/steal-brainrot). This is a **data artifact the code faithfully propagates, not a code bug.** Throughout this report I trust **per-`genre_l2` breakdowns and individual game rows** over headline cluster numbers. Crucially, the keyboard mega-title does **not** appear in the cooking/food/restaurant files — those openness reads are clean.
> - **Score compression.** The opportunity score's demand component caps at ~100k CCU and freshness caps at 2 fresh winners, so cooking (83.8), food (85.9), restaurant (83.5), RNG (80.8) all cluster in a ~10-pt band despite ~8x demand spread. **Do not rank lanes by the headline score** — rank by per-genre fragmentation + count of independent 1k+ winners, as done above.
> - **Verified metric math.** An independent recompute of demand_ccu, leader_share, HHI, winners_1k, visits/day, and ccu/1k_visits from the raw game arrays matched the stored analysis in all 10 files (0 mismatches) — the arithmetic is trustworthy; only the *input set* is contaminated.
> - **Minor real code fix noted:** ISO timestamps carry 7-digit fractional seconds; `datetime.fromisoformat` only handles these on Python 3.11+ (this env is 3.14, so it's fine). On ≤3.10 freshness/staleness would silently fall to 0 — normalize subseconds to 6 digits if the scanner is ever run on older interpreters.
